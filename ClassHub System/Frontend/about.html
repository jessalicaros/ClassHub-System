About ClassHub

ClassHub is a comprehensive teacher portal designed to simplify and enhance the management of student data, attendance tracking, and grade computation. It provides educators with an intuitive interface for managing student profiles, monitoring attendance, and automating grade calculations, all in one place. By integrating SMS notifications, ClassHub ensures that parents are always informed about their child's attendance and academic progress, fostering better communication between teachers and families.

Empowering Teachers with Seamless Attendance & Student Management

ClassHub is designed to simplify the way teachers manage student attendance, grades, and profiles, enhancing the educational experience for both teachers and students.

  • Real-time attendance tracking with automated parent notifications.
  • Comprehensive student profiles including academic and behavioral data.
  • Efficient report generation for grades, attendance, and behavior.
  • AI-powered assistance to streamline attendance queries and scheduling.

With ClassHub, teachers can focus more on teaching while reducing administrative overhead, ensuring that both educators and students thrive in a modern, connected educational environment.
